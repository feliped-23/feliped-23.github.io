My goals

Grow as a design/test engineer, contribute to sustainable energy systems, and keep shipping projects that balance performance, manufacturability, and cost. Near-term: broaden prototyping depth and publish more write-ups.

My passions

Clean energy, clever mechanisms, and well-instrumented experiments. Outside of work: hiking, winter sports, and learning by building. If it involves a fixture, a test plan, or a graph, I’m in.
