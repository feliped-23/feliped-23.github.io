My story

On the facade I am an Integrated Engineer and a Clean tech enthusiast, however I identify strongest with my unique upbringing and my urge for constant growth.

I grew up moving around the world, on average living 3 years in each country. My dads job took my family around the globe and it was a defining experience throughout my childhood. Although it brought a lot of challenges, especially for a young boy, it was the best thing that ever happened to me. I was exposed to many drastically different cultures and people. I developed high quality social skills as a survival mechanism. I became accustomed to living outside my comfort zone.
