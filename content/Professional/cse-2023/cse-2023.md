# Conference on Sustainability in Engineering (CSE 2023)

**Type:** Conference
**Period:** January 2023
**Organization:** Conference on Sustainability in Engineering — UBC

## Summary

Attended the four-day national Conference on Sustainability in Engineering, bringing together 200+ students from 43 institutions across Canada. Engaged through case competitions, panels, and workshops focused on integrating sustainability and equity into engineering practice. The conference introduced concepts of two-eyed seeing, co-creation, and decolonization as frameworks for enhancing the engineering design process.

## Key Topics Covered

- Sustainable engineering design methodologies
- Two-eyed seeing and Indigenous knowledge integration in engineering
- Co-creation and community-based design approaches
- Decolonization of engineering practice and pedagogy
- Systems thinking for sustainability
- Case competitions on real-world engineering sustainability challenges

## Key Achievements

- One of 200+ student delegates from 43 Canadian institutions
- Participated in hands-on sustainable engineering workshops and case competitions
- Integrated sustainability and equity frameworks into engineering design thinking
