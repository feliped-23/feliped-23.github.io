# 3D Printing Shop Manager — UBC Faculty of Integrated Engineering

**Type:** Work Experience
**Period:** Mar 2023 – Dec 2023
**Organization:** UBC Faculty of Integrated Engineering — Vancouver, BC

## Summary

Operated the faculty 3D printing farm for UBC's Integrated Engineering program, printing high-quality parts for student projects while maintaining printer uptime across 8+ FDM and resin machines. Developed strong intuition for printability of complex geometries and optimized slicer settings for structural and aesthetic requirements. Advised students on GD&T callouts for functional interfaces, diagnosing tolerance failures and recommending fit class adjustments to minimize reprint cycles.

## Key Achievements

- Maintained 8+ FDM and resin printers at full capacity for continuous student and faculty use
- Diagnosed and repaired electromechanical printer failures: extruder, motion system, and resin cure issues
- Advised students on GD&T callouts (press fits, clearance fits, datum-relative features) to achieve working assemblies with minimal reprint cycles
- Developed deep intuition for printability, support strategies, and tolerances across diverse part geometries
- Maintained printer calibration, cleaning, and consumable inventory for consistent print quality under heavy student usage
- Delivered parts on tight timelines aligned with academic project deadlines

## Tools & Tech

FDM 3D printing, resin (SLA) 3D printing, slicing software, GD&T, electromechanical troubleshooting, printer maintenance and calibration, manufacturing quality
