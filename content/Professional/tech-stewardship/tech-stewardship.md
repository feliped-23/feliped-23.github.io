# Technology Stewardship Practice Program

**Type:** Certificate / Micro-credential
**Period:** May 2024
**Organization:** Technology Stewardship Practice Program

## Summary

12-hour micro-credential focused on purposeful, responsible, inclusive, and regenerative technology stewardship. Trained in applying ethical, inclusive, and sustainable frameworks to technology development, and built perspective on responsible innovation and the role of engineers as stewards of new ventures and emerging technologies.

## Key Topics Covered

- Ethical frameworks for technology development
- Inclusive design and equitable technology access
- Sustainable and regenerative approaches to engineering innovation
- Responsible innovation practices
- Engineer's role as technology steward in society
- Frameworks for evaluating societal impact of emerging technologies
