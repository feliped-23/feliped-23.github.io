# Jetski Tour Guide — Vancouver Water Adventures

**Type:** Work Experience
**Period:** May 2025 – Aug 2025
**Organization:** Vancouver Water Adventures — Vancouver, BC

## Summary

Led guided jetski tours through Vancouver's Howe Sound for groups of up to 21 riders, combining safety management, real-time risk assessment, and guest experience delivery in dynamic ocean conditions. Adapted tour pacing, communication style, and teaching approach across groups with widely varied skill levels and personalities. Also performed routine mechanical inspections and on-water troubleshooting (clogged impellers, fluid checks, mechanical faults) to maintain fleet uptime. Earned multiple five-star customer reviews.

## Key Achievements

- Led on-water tours of up to 21 jetskis simultaneously through Vancouver harbour and Howe Sound
- Applied real-time risk assessment and proactive hazard recognition in high-pressure, dynamic marine conditions
- Adapted tour approach across diverse international client groups — varying skill levels, languages, and personalities
- Performed corrective maintenance including clogged impellers, fluid checks, and on-water mechanical troubleshooting
- Conducted pre-tour safety briefings and equipment checks, ensuring full regulatory compliance before launch
- Managed dockside operations: jetski, paddleboard, and kayak rentals
- Multiple five-star customer reviews citing guide personality, safety, and unforgettable experience

## Tools & Tech

Jetski operation and maintenance, marine safety protocols, client management, group leadership, on-water risk assessment, impeller maintenance, pre-launch safety briefings
